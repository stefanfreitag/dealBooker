
## Basic elements  
When trading you need to specify this information
1. Type of deal type: buy/ sell
2. Quantity:  a positive value, e.g. 100
3. Unit: Watt/ Kilowatt/ Megawatt/ Gigawatt
4. Product: Within day/ day ahead/ bom/ rom
